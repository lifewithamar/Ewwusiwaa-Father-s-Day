To My Husband on Father’s Day

Today I celebrate the man I love, A father sent as a gift from above. Strong in heart, gentle in care, Always present, always there. You lead our family with wisdom and grace, Bringing warmth and joy to every place. With every sacrifice, both big and small, You give your very best to us all. The love you show, so pure and true, Makes our house a home because of you. A devoted husband, a father so rare, A constant reminder of how deeply you care. Thank you for the laughter, guidance, and light, For turning our struggles into strength and might. Today we honour the wonderful man you are, Our protector, our blessing, our shining star. On this Father’s Day, I want to say, We cherish and love you more every day. May joy and favour follow all you do, Because our world is better because of you. Happy Father’s Day, my love. You are appreciated beyond words. ❤️

Thank you for all the lessons you teach, the support you offer, and the firm foundation you continue to build for our family. Happy Father's Day!

- With respect and love, Becky.
